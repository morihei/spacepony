::: LIFE STORY :::

As part of the Norman & Nielsen group I'm a certified User Experience Designer, a passionate Visual Artist and Lead Front-end Developer currently based in Shanghai. Working with companies based in U.S, China, Sweden and Italy during the last 12 years I gained a wide experience in the Creative & IT industry. This gave me the opportunity to work on various projects covering some of the following specialities:


- Creation of site maps, user flow, high fidelity wireframes, and interactive prototypes

- Design of visually trendy and functionally efficient prototypes accordingly to the results of the research

- Painting the big picture of the product with trendy UI patterns

::: ABOUT ME :::

Here you can find my latest explorations and futuristic concepts that make me tick. As I become more specialized in various design areas of the known and unknown, I enjoy exploring futuristic and abstract designs and concepts for new media so you don’t ever have to.


On the side as a tech nerd, I love to keep myself abreast with the latest design tools and keep myself ahead of the curve. To push the brain cells to the edges of logic and abstraction gives him the most satisfaction in every project.
