01 :::

The process

As a young EV startup with a lot of passion for the next cutting edge technology trends, NIO had a vision of creating a infotainment system that would beat any current system on the market. The race was on to create an impeccable console screen experience which would become the next trend setter in the market of modern EV cars.

02 :::

The definition

Defining a product is never easy. Especially in the automotive industry this task can seem like a worm hole without an end. The process of throwing out what is overkill for the layouts and apps, as to throwing in what will make the most impactful user journey was full of exceptional challenges for the team.

03 :::

Ideation

As they say; "Everything starts with an idea". In this case it was no different. The ideation process took the creatives through a mine field of engineering obstacles that needed to be avoided for the prototype creation to take it's place.

04 :::

Prototype

After a long immersive prototyping phase the product is slowly getting ready to be tested on certain in-house laboratory facilities to get that crucial user feedback needed.
