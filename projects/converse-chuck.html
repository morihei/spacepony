::: PROJECT :::

Converse - Heart of the City

::: CATEGORY :::

  • Video|

  • Motion Design

::: YEAR :::

2020

::: SYNOPSIS :::

Plus Minus Creative was tasked by Converse to craft a fluid and visually striking motion edit, "Heart of the City." The campaign highlighted the brand's legendary 70s sneakers, weaving them into the fabric of three iconic cities: Paris, Los Angeles, and Shanghai. Space Pony's mission was to develop a seamless, captivating and smooth video that would make a lasting impression on Wechat's digital audience.

01 :::

The Heart of the City

The story of three most recognizable cities in the world would each highlight a special shoe model tailored by Converse and bring it closer to young audiences in China. The animation would blend a fresh urban perspective of the Chinese youth and recognizable Converse design.

02 :::

The second part of the campaign

A while after the first version, Converse revisited their campaign with a dynamic animation highlighting key shoe models. Pony swiftly delivered smooth, falling animations tailored to the specific layout of each presentation.
